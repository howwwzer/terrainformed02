THE CREATOR

Annika

This site and its research are created by Annika, a Michigander with a love for space and Earth. This project was created to educate people about the advancement of the commercial space industry and its potential negative outcomes. Annika urgers their readers to think critically about the implications of privatized space travel on people, the environment, and humanity's relationship with space.
