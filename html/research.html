Who are the stakeholders?

MAMA

MAMA (Michigan Aerospace Manufacturers Association) is a lobbyist group that aims to "Position Michigan as a viable space state by encouraging public-private partnerships to develop a space ecosystem that will positively affect the standard of living for all residents.‚Äù Based in Sterling Heights, this organization facilitates business development, education, networking opportunities, legislative updates, industry events in the hopes that it can establish Michigan as the next space state.

MAMA has three primary initiatives. The one that is relevant for this project is the first: their Michigan Launch Initiative. This initiative is designed to establish horizontal and vertical LEO launch sites and a control center in the state of Michigan. Currently, MAMA is looking to Upper Peninsula to launch by 2026.
